Smugglers Cove Rum List

Martin Cate from Smuggler’s Cove has organized an excellent list of rums categorize by geographical origin, still type, and age. In the book, he describes several categories into which the bottles he recommends can be grouped. Of these categories, 8 have an associated number that he uses in his recipes rather than describing specific bottles. I’ve copied this list here for quick reference behind the bar. Items in bold will be easier to find. Italics means overproof.
